Border Collie

Border Collie is a medium-sized, well-proportioned dog. The fur can be fine or of medium length, the most common color being white with black, although there are other colors along with white - e.g. brown, blue, blackbird, red and tricolor. Adult males are about 53cm; females slightly less.

Beagle

These solid and bold puppies have a gentle expression and a compact and athletic look. They have short, dense and weather-resistant fur, in a wide range of colors and patterns (for details, see the breed standard). They have a height of 33-40cm and weigh about 10-11kg.

Husky Siberian

With double fur, medium length, with raised ears and a bushy tail, this breed can have all colors and spots, including white, with some obvious patterns visible. Adult males have a height of 53-60 centimeters, and females 51-56 centimeters. Adult males weigh 20-27 kilograms, and adult females 16-23 kg.

Homeless dogs

There are millions of abandoned dogs on the streets around the world, and combating this alarming phenomenon seems to be a daunting task, especially because of the easy breeding of dogs and the irresponsibility of many people. Dogs are taken to shelters due to overcrowding, while those who remain on the streets lead a life of fear, hunger, cold and physical abuse. Adopting a stray dog will save their lives, giving them a second chance to have a home and live a dignified and happy life. Also, if you have enough free space, you should also get a second dog to complete a perfect family. Several studies have shown the positive effect that a dog has on human health. Not only is it proven that those children who come in contact with an animal will develop the necessary defenses against allergies, but it is also known that this coexistence with an animal can improve your cardiovascular health, help fight stress and relax you.
So let's save some lives!

Dry food is an economical food for dogs. Unlike wet food, it can be stored more easily, helps maintain the dog's dental health, provides a regular stool and the cost, per serving, is lower. Before choosing a type of dry food for your dog, we recommend that you read the ingredients of the products carefully and make sure that the manufacturer uses quality raw materials in their composition.
